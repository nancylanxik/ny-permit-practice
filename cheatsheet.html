NY Permit Test — Cheat Sheet

One page · verified vs. official manual

⚠️ Drill these first — your common misses

  • Fire hydrant = 15 ft (never 5/6/8)
  • Dim high beams = 500 ft oncoming · 200 ft following
  • School zone = 15–20 mph (NOT 25 — that's a city street)
  • ORANGE sign = construction / work zone (yellow = hazard)
  • School bus, red lights = stop 20 ft back, both directions
  • NYC default speed = 25 mph (rest of NY = 55)
  • Pass on the right ONLY when car ahead turns left
  • Double solid yellow = no pass (may cross to turn into a driveway)
  • Junior license 9pm–5am: NYC = no driving; LI/upstate = home↔work/school
  • Refuse chemical test = 1-yr revoke

★ Numbers

  • 25 mph — NYC default speed
  • 55 mph — rest-of-NY default
  • 15–20 mph — school zones
  • 2 sec — following distance (3–4 behind big trucks)
  • 100 ft — signal before turn
  • 500 ft — dim high beams (oncoming)
  • 200 ft — dim (following) / don't tailgate emergency veh.
  • .08 DWI · .18 agg · .02 under-21
  • 20/14 — questions / to pass
  • 11 pts in 18 mo → suspension
  • $1,000 damage → report crash to DMV

🅿️ Parking — keep clear by

  • 15 ft — fire hydrant
  • 20 ft — crosswalk at intersection
  • 20 ft — fire-station driveway (75 ft opposite)
  • 30 ft — traffic light, STOP/YIELD, safety zone
  • 50 ft — railroad crossing
Ladder 15→20→30→50 · meters 5→6→10→15

Park on a hill

  • Uphill + curb → wheels away from curb
  • Downhill → wheels toward curb
  • No curb → wheels toward road edge
  • Always set parking brake + park/1st gear

🚦 Traffic lights

  • Flashing red = STOP sign
  • Flashing yellow = slow, caution
  • Steady yellow = stop if safe
  • Green arrow = protected turn
  • Dark signal = 4-way stop

↔️ Right-of-way

  • 4-way tie → car on the right
  • Turning left → yield oncoming + peds
  • In roundabout/circle → you have it
  • From driveway → yield to all
  • Blind pedestrian → always yield

🌙 Night & weather

  • Headlights: ½hr after sunset–½hr before sunrise, & with wipers
  • Dim high beams 500 ft oncoming, 200 ft following
  • Fog/rain/snow → low beams
Bad weather = LOW beams

↗️ Passing & lines

  • Normally pass on the left
  • Right only when car ahead turns left
  • Return when you see it in your mirror
  • Broken line = pass OK · double solid yellow = no pass (cross only to turn into driveway)
  • School bus red lights → stop 20 ft back, both ways

🖐️ Hand signals (left arm)

  • Straight out = left turn
  • Bent up = right turn
  • Bent down = stop / slow

🍺 Alcohol & drugs

  • DWI (21+): .08+ · 6-mo revoke
  • DWAI: .05–.07 · 90-day susp.
  • Aggravated DWI: .18+ · 1-yr
  • Under 21 (Zero Tol.): .02+
  • Refuse test → 1-yr revoke
  • Only time lowers BAC

🗽 NYC-specific

  • No right on red unless posted
  • Default speed 25 mph
  • Phone/text: up to $200 + 5 pts
  • Move Over Law: room for stopped emergency vehicles

🚸 Junior license (under 18)

  • 9 PM–5 AM curfew by region:
  • NYC → no driving
  • LI / Upstate → solo, home↔work/school
  • 1 passenger under 21 (unless family)
  • Permit = always supervised (21+)

⚠️ Emergencies

  • Skid → off gas, steer where you want to go
  • Brakes fail → pump first
  • Blowout → grip wheel, off gas, slow
  • Miss exit → next exit (never back up)
  • Stuck in crosswalk → wait, don't reverse

🚴 Sharing the road

  • No sidewalk → walk facing traffic
  • Bikes ride with traffic; need front white + red rear light at night; give 3 ft
  • Slow-moving emblem = orange triangle
  • Trucks: stay where you see their mirrors

🅿️ Seat belts & kids

  • Under 4 → child safety seat
  • 4–7 → booster + lap/shoulder belt
  • Driver responsible for under-16; 16+ responsible for self
  • NY = primary enforcement
